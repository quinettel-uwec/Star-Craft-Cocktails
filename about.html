About Us

Star Craft Cocktails was founded in 2022. Owner and founder Sheila Quinette saw a market for unique and fun gift ideas. The company mission is to create and give customers the experience of creating delicious cocktails with natural ingredients for themselves or their loved ones.

Meet the Owner

Sheila Quinette is Milwaukee, WI based and lives with her husband and children. Despite working a full time job and having a family, Sheila manages Star Craft Cocktails, fills orders, and is creating new products and listings all the time.
